**Last Updated:** January 12, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Halóre ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

**Booking Information:**

**Contact Information:**
- Phone number (optional)
- Mailing address (optional)

**Payment Information:**

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type, operating system, and version
- Device identifier and unique advertising ID
- Mobile network information

**Usage Data:**
- Pages or features you access and the time spent on them
- Links you click
- Search queries and booking history
- Crash reports and performance metrics

**Location Data:**
- Location information associated with selected service locations
- Your precise location is not collected unless explicitly authorized by you

**Push Notifications:**
- We collect data about whether you open or engage with push notifications

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Service Provision:** Creating and managing your account, processing bookings, and providing customer support
- **Communication:** Sending booking confirmations, appointment reminders, and responding to inquiries
- **Push Notifications:** Notifying you about booking status, special offers, and important updates
- **Personalization:** Customizing your experience and remembering your preferences
- **Analytics:** Analyzing app usage patterns to improve functionality and user experience
- **Security:** Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities and unauthorized access
- **Legal Compliance:** Complying with applicable laws and regulations
- **Marketing:** With your consent, sending promotional content and newsletters (you can opt-out anytime)

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Security measures include:

- **End-to-End Encryption:** Sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols (SSL/TLS)
- **Firebase Security:** Data stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Authentication is protected by Google's security infrastructure
- **Secure Storage:** Sensitive data like authentication tokens are stored in secure device storage using `flutter_secure_storage`
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Updates:** We maintain up-to-date security patches and monitoring

However, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## 6. Data Retention
